Upcoming Grand Prix - F1&MotoGP

Event Overview

The 2025 Formula 1 Gran Premio de la Ciudad de México is scheduled to take place on Sunday, October 26, 2025, at the Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City. This race marks the 20th round of the 2025 Formula One World Championship and will be contested over 71 laps of the 4.304 km (2.674 mi) circuit, totaling a race distance of 305.584 km (190.5 mi). Wikipedia

Full Weekend Schedule

The event spans from October 24 to October 26, 2025, featuring the following schedule:

  • Friday, October 24:

    • Practice 1: 12:30 – 13:30 (Local Time)

  • Saturday, October 25:

    • Practice 3: 12:30 – 13:30 (Local Time)

    • Qualifying: 17:00 – 18:00 (Local Time)

  • Sunday, October 26:

    • Race: 15:00 – 17:00 (Local Time)

Please note that Mexico City operates on Central Standard Time (CST), which is UTC -6. Formula 1® - The Official F1® Website

Circuit Details

The Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez is renowned for its high-altitude setting, situated over 2,200 meters (7,200 feet) above sea level. This elevation significantly affects engine performance and aerodynamics, presenting unique challenges for teams and drivers. The circuit layout includes the iconic Foro Sol stadium section, where fans create an electrifying atmosphere, and the Peraltada corner, a high-speed left-hander that has been modified in recent years for safety improvements. Formula 1® - The Official F1® Website

Atmosphere & Fan Engagement

The Mexico City Grand Prix is celebrated for its vibrant atmosphere, blending high-speed racing with Mexican culture. The event has been a favorite among fans, teams, and drivers alike, with every event sold out since its return to the calendar in 2015. In 2024, a record 405,000 people attended the festivities, underscoring the event's popularity. Formula 1® - The Official F1® Website

Fans can look forward to various activities, including driver parades, live music performances, and cultural exhibitions, all contributing to the festive ambiance. The Foro Sol area, in particular, is known for its immersive fan experiences and interactive displays.

Championship Situation after United States GP

After Max Verstappen's dominant weekend at the United States Grand Prix, he is now only 40 points behind championship leader, Oscar Piastri. While second in the championship Lando Norris is 12 points back in second with only George Russell in 4th left mathematically in championship contention. 

Rookie of the year is set to be going the way of Kimi Antonelli who is 50 points ahead of nearest rival Isack Hadjar.
